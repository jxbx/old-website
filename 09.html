Sketchbook Work

I use observational sketchbook work to explore space, form and composition. I prefer to work on a small page using hard materials, usually pen and marker. In some of my work I aim to build up complex images based on multiple fragmentrary views of a scene in front of me, creating a montage effect. In other drawings I try to define a large space or environment by blocking out well-defined areas with coloured pencils or markers.
