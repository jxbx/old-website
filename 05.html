Witness the Wackness

Witness the Wackness is a CD of my own music, produced in limited numbers and intended as an experiment in incorporating illustration into packaging design.

I wanted to create a piece of illustrative work which could be handled and touched, so I used bright colours and distinctive textures, including felt, and several card and paper stocks, to make the process of opening the packaging into a surprising, exploratory experience, like opening a carefully wrapped present. I incorporated a wide range of illustrative approaches into the piece, creating a combination of photographed and hand-drawn elements for the packaging and an abstract design inspired by textile designs by Maija Isola for the disc.
