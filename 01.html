Magda Finds Her Keys

Magda Finds Her Keys is a 28-page illustrated story designed as a webcomic. In this piece I tried to transpose an assertive, kinetic visual style onto an uneventful story to create an unusual fantasy. The scenario—where a woman searches for her keys in her handbag—is a starting point for an unconfined exploration of visual narrative. Throughout the piece I sought to offset precise, detailed compositions against freer designs which break away from the narrative in playful, digressive ways, whilst using repeated images and visual structures to create a kind of continuity.
